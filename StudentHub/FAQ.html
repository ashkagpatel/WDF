Frequently Ask Questions


The FAQ page provides answers to common questions related to the StudentHub Portal.

FAQs

  1. How do I register?
    Ans: Click on the Register page and fill out the registration form.

  2. How can I log in?
    Ans: Enter your email or student ID and password on the Login page.

  3. How can I check my attendance?
    Ans: Open the Attendance module from the Dashboard.

  4. How can I view my result?
    Ans: Select the Result option from the Dashboard.

  5. Who should I contact for support?
    Ans: Visit the Contact Us page or contact the StudentHub Support Team.
